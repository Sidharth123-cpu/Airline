Our Eco-Friendly Aircraft
Our Boeing 737 fleet is designed to offer the greatest flexibility, reliability and efficiency in the single-aisle market. Every aircraft will feature the new Boeing Sky Interior, highlighted by modern sculpted sidewalls and window reveals, LED lighting that enhances the sense of spaciousness and larger pivoting overhead storage bins.
With the use of alternative fuels and removed excess weight to reduce emissions, our aircraft is the most eco-friendly. With a noise footprint almost seven times smaller than single-aisle and wide-body aircraft. They are also as fuel efficient as an eco-friendly car, producing emissions that are 30-40% lower than other aircraft.
