What is AstraPlus?
With AstraPlus, one trip lead you to the next one.AstraPlus is designed to give perks and upgrades when you fly with us. Accumulate miles and redeem them for air travel and increased benefits, such as travel class upgrades, airport lounge access, fast track access, and priority bookings. When you travel, you will earn miles which can be redeemed for award flights and more once you've earned enough.Your miles never expire and can take you to more than 1,000 destinations. Plus, there are no blackout dates on any Astra Airlines flight. Join today and let the adventure begin.
